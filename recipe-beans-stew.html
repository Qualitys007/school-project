Nigerian Beans Stew

Origin: Nigeria

Cost: ~$6 (6 servings)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups beans
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tbsp palm oil or vegetable oil
  • Spices: salt, pepper, chili

Steps

  1. Boil beans until soft.
  2. Saut√© onion in oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce and spices, simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Mix beans with sauce and cook 5 more minutes.
