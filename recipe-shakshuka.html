Shakshuka

Origin: Middle East / North Africa

Cost: ~$5 (4 servings)

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Spices: paprika, cumin, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Saut√© onion and garlic in oil until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Crack eggs into the sauce, cover, and cook until set.
