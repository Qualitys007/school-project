Fried Rice

Origin: China

Cost: ~$4 (4 servings)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 green onions

Steps

  1. Cook rice and let cool.
  2. Scramble egg in pan.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry.
  4. Add rice and soy sauce, stir until combined.
